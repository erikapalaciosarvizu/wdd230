Membership Benefits


NP (Nonprofit) Membership:

• Free of Charge: Enjoy the basic benefits of chamber membership without any cost.
• Networking Opportunities: Connect with other nonprofit organizations to share resources and collaborate on common goals.
• Basic Listing: Get listed on the chamber's website.

Bronze Membership:

Cost: $50.00 • Networking Events: Participate in exclusive networking events to build relationships with fellow businesses and potential clients.
• Online Promotion: Basic listing upgraded to include a brief business description.
• Educational Workshops: Attend specialized workshops and training sessions at a discounted rate.
• Event Discounts: Receive discounts on chamber-sponsored events.

Silver Membership:

Cost: $100.00 • Enhanced Visibility: Featured placement on the chamber's website and promotional materials.
• Event Sponsorship Opportunities: Showcase your business by sponsoring chamber events, gaining exposure to a broader audience.
• Business Referrals: Priority referrals from the chamber and fellow members..
• Access to Premium Resources: Exclusive access to premium resources, industry reports, and market insights.
• Training Workshops: Complimentary attendance at select training workshops.


Gold Membership:

Cost: $200.00 • Top-Tier Networking: Connect with influential business leaders and decision-makers in the community through exclusive events.
• Priority Event Access: VIP treatment and priority access at chamber events, maximizing your networking opportunities.
• Customized Marketing: Personalized marketing support, including featured articles, social media promotion, and tailored marketing campaigns.
• Board Representation: Opportunity for representation on the chamber's board, allowing your business a voice in shaping local business policies.
• Spotlight Position: Prime advertising space on the chamber's home page.
