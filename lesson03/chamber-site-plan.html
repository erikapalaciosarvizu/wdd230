Overview

Purpose

The main objective of the Dolores Hidalgo Chamber of Commerce is to promote the economic and commercial development of our community. Our website has two main purposes: Local Business Promotion and Business Resources and Support.

Scenarios

-What are the next events organized by the Chamber that promote networking between companies?

-Where can I find contact information for members of the board of directors of the Dolores Hidalgo Chamber of Commerce?

-What has been the population growth in the Dolores Hidalgo area in recent years?
